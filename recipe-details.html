Recipe: Manto

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wonton wrappers or dough

Instructions:

  1. Mix ground beef with onion, garlic, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Fill wonton wrappers with the mixture and steam for 20 minutes.
  3. Serve with yogurt and garlic sauce.

Recipe: Kebab

Ingredients:

  • 500g lamb or beef, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers (soaked in water for 30 minutes)

Instructions:

  1. Mix the meat with onion, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Thread the meat onto the skewers.
  3. Grill for 8-10 minutes on medium-high heat.
  4. Serve with bread, rice, and salad.

Recipe: Sheer Chai

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons black tea leaves
  • Cardamom, cinnamon, and saffron to taste

    • Instructions:

    1. Boil the water and add the black tea leaves.

Recipe: Sheer Khorma

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup vermicelli
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup dates, chopped
  • 1/4 cup almonds and pistachios
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron

Instructions:

  1. Roast the vermicelli in a little ghee or butter until golden brown.
  2. In a pot, heat milk and add sugar and saffron. Stir until sugar dissolves.
  3. Add the roasted vermicelli and cook for about 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in chopped dates, almonds, and pistachios.
  5. Serve warm or chilled.

Recipe: Ferini

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon rose water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions:

  1. Mix rice flour with milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  2. Add sugar, rose water, and cardamom powder once it thickens.
  3. Continue to cook until the mixture reaches a pudding-like consistency.
  4. Serve warm or chilled.

Recipe: Baklava

Ingredients:

  • 1 package phyllo dough
  • 2 cups mixed nuts (walnuts, pistachios, almonds)
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon rose water (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Layer phyllo dough in a baking dish, brushing each sheet with melted butter.
  3. Sprinkle a layer of mixed nuts between the phyllo layers.
  4. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, then cut into diamond shapes.
  5. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Meanwhile, make the syrup by combining sugar, water, and lemon juice in a pot and simmering for 10 minutes.
  7. Pour the hot syrup over the baked baklava and let it soak for a few hours before serving.

Submitted by: Noorani
